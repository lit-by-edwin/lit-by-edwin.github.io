Important Information and Key Dates

For any questions related to the competition please read The Uber Launch Challenge FAQ here. If your question is not answered in the FAQ, please email Anthony Makragelidis at president@subsoc.com.au.

Your team is required to submit your final presentation by 11:59PM AEST Sunday 7th May by emailing the presentation to Anthony Makragelidis at president@subsoc.com.au.
The subject line of the email should contain the phrase "Uber Launch Challenge Submission", your team name and university (e.g. Uber Launch Challenge Team Ridesharing University of Sydney). Each team is only allowed to enter one submission for consideration.

Following submissions, your team will be sent an email informing you of whether you have advanced to pitch your idea in front of a judging panel at the Campus Heats. Our goal is for as many teams as possible to have the opportunity to pitch their ideas at the Campus Heats, however, given the large number of entrants, this may not always be possible. For teams that do not advance to Campus Heats, written feedback by way of email will be provided. Teams that advance to Campus Heats will be allocated a presentation time between 10 May and 14 May.

The winning team from each of the 8 Campus Heats will advance to the Finals and have the opportunity pitch their strategy to the Uber Leadership Team in Sydney on Monday 22 May. To advance to the Finals, at least two team members must be available to travel to Sydney and pitch in person on 22 May.

Finally, please note that your ongoing participation in the competition is subject to these Terms and Conditions.
